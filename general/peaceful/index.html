    Peaceful Mode¶

    Have ever playing Minecraft in Peaceful Mode? Like it's name, peaceful, means no Hostile Mob, which is good. You doesn't need to kill any zombie again, die because of Creeper's Explosion, poison from cave spider, etc. Any neutral mob are now friendly, they will not attacking you when you punch them accidentally.

    Peaceful give you regeneration (Likely level 2), which is you doesn't need care about your health (But still you can die). Player's hunger never depleate, means that you don't need to kill any passive mob again (~~Vegetarian will happy~~)

    Most of you think, the peaceful was the easiest mode in Minecraft. Well... NO. Although you doesn't need to kill hostile mob, but some mob drop useful item, such as Blaze Rod and Ender Pearl for go to the End. But in Peaceful mode, you can't go to the End, means no elytra and shulker.

    Health & Hunger¶

    Regeneration on peaceful mode was faster than another mode. Even though, you still can die because of fall damage, fell into void, or some general death. Any explosion doesn't give you damage, but still give you knockback.

    You hunger never deplete. That's mean, you can run forever without worrying about hunger. So no more eatting.

    Beating Minecraft¶

    One goal of playing Minecraft is to kill Ender Dragon. Killing the Ender Dragon will allow us go to End City, which is gives us the best item, Elytra and Shulker shells for Shulker Box.

    But, Blaze and Enderman will not spawn in Peaceful Mode. Meaning Blaze Rod (Blaze Powder) and Ender Pearl is unobtainable? Ender Pearl is still obtainable, thanks to Villager. But blaze rod is unobtainable because you need Blaze to get it. So, you can't craft Eye of Ender.

    Beating Minecraft is Impossible, or not?

    Did you remember, End Portal on stronghold can filled with eye? That's mean, we need to find seed with 12-Eyes Stronghold. So beat the Ender Dragon is possible.
